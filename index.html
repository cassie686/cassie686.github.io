Hi, I am

Cassie Liu

Hi :) I'm Cassie Liu / 刘笛清 (Liu, Diqing). I hold a Master’s degree in Mathematical Economics and Econometrics from Boston University and a Bachelor's degree in Insurance Accounting from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics in Chengdu, China.

I am an Energy & Sustainability Analyst specializing in power markets, policy analysis, financial and quantitative modeling. My work focuses on policy impact assessment, electricity market forecasting, and decarbonization strategies. I have worked with senior government officials, industry leaders, and investors to support clean energy transitions and sustainability initiatives.

With a strong foundation in economics and data-driven analysis, I assess market dynamics, regulatory risks, and investment strategies. I have also contributed to research on energy efficiency in data centers and the financial implications of carbon pricing. I thrive in fast-paced environments, leveraging quantitative insights to solve complex, high-impact challenges.

Cassie Liu

Hi :) I'm Cassie Liu / 刘笛清 (Liu, Diqing). I built this website because a résumé often falls short in capturing the depth of my work and interests. Here, I share updates on my projects, research, and ideas.

I hold a Master’s degree in Mathematical Economics and Econometrics from Boston University and a Bachelor's degree in Insurance Accounting from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics in Chengdu, China.

My work spans energy markets, policy analysis, and financial modeling, applying data-driven insights to real-world challenges. I analyze power markets, regulatory risks, and decarbonization strategies, working with government agencies, investors, and industry leaders to support clean energy transitions.

My research also explores AI’s role in market efficiency, data center sustainability, and carbon finance. I thrive in fast-paced, analytical environments, where data, policy, and strategy converge. This site reflects my work ethic and curiosity. Feel free to explore.

Pro Bono Consulting Project

Driving Growth Through Strategy & Analytics

Beyond my research and academic experiences, I have also worked on pro bono consulting projects, applying data-driven strategy and market analysis to help early-stage companies refine their growth trajectories. Due to confidentiality agreements, I cannot disclose specific company details, but I can share my key contributions to these engagements.

In the most recent project, I collaborated with a high-growth technology company to develop a structured market expansion and revenue growth strategy. My work focused on:

  • Competitive & Market Analysis – Evaluated the company's positioning in a rapidly evolving industry by benchmarking against competitors and assessing go-to-market strategies.
  • Pricing & Monetization Strategy – Developed a value-based pricing framework, aligning product differentiation with customer willingness to pay.
  • Revenue Modeling & Valuation – Built financial models to project long-term revenue growth, investment needs, and potential valuation scenarios.
  • Strategic Growth Planning – Outlined a phased market entry strategy, identifying key industries for expansion and optimizing customer acquisition pathways.

This project reinforced my ability to bridge business strategy with quantitative analysis, leveraging financial modeling, pricing optimization, and competitive intelligence to support data-driven decision-making. More importantly, it highlighted the importance of adaptable strategies in fast-changing industries—whether in SaaS, digital transformation, or enterprise technology.

I’m always eager to explore how analytical rigor and strategic insight can drive sustainable growth. If you’re interested in discussing business strategy, market expansion, or SaaS economics, feel free to connect!
